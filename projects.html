Recipe Book

visit the site!

Cooking has always been a passion of mine, so I decided to use my skills to share it with the world. I worked together with a couple friends to put together a database of recipes and created a place where each recipe is clearly visible and easy to access.

We are still working on the finishing touches but here is a quick sneak peek:




Sort the recipes by Meal, Cusine or Prep Time. Easy recipe is a rotating card that displays the ingredients and steps when you hover over it. Select the ingredients you need for each recipe and build a grocery list. For easy access, the list will be copied to your clipboard.


Select the ingredients you already have and find recipes that use those ingredients


Plan an entire meal using a preset, or by clicking on a recipe to switch it out
